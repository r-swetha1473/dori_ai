Frequently asked questions

Get answers to your most pressing questions about how Dori AI improves quality, reduces defects, enhances setup, and ensures compliance across your operations.

How does Dori help reduce defects and improve quality?

Dori uses computer vision AI to detect defects in real-time, monitoring production lines continuously without fatigue. The system automatically identifies quality issues that human inspectors might miss, especially during high-speed manufacturing. By catching defects early, manufacturers can address root causes faster, reducing waste and improving overall product quality.

Can Dori support compliance and audit readiness?

Yes, Dori creates an automated digital trail of manufacturing processes that supports compliance with industry regulations. The platform documents all production steps, quality checks, and process adherence, making audit preparation significantly easier. Historical data is easily retrievable, and reports can be generated automatically to demonstrate compliance with specific requirements.

Which quality metrics can Dori track?

Dori tracks comprehensive quality metrics including defect rates, first-pass yield, scrap rates, rework percentages, process capability indices (Cpk), Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE), Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF), and customer complaint rates. The platform can be customized to track industry-specific metrics relevant to your manufacturing processes.
